07

Principles

The four rules every screen obeys.

A

Photos breathe

Buyers buy on the photo, not the copy. Imagery is square, edge-to-edge, lazy-loaded — captions stay out of its way.

B

Type does the work

Limited palette, strong hierarchy. A serif headline and a mono price carry a card — no shadows, no clutter.

C

Built for a Tecno

Dark by default to save battery. Fast first paint, 44px hit targets, nothing below 13px. Cracked-screen friendly.

D

Safer than a DM

In-app messaging, verified shops, visible report & expiry. Trust signals are part of the layout, not an afterthought.
